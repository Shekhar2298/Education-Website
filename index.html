Why Comprehensive Sex Education Matters

Evidence shows that comprehensive sex education leads to better health outcomes and healthier relationships

Improved Health Outcomes

Comprehensive sex education is associated with lower rates of STIs, unintended pregnancies, and sexual violence.

  • Reduced STI transmission
  • Lower teen pregnancy rates
  • Increased contraceptive use

Healthier Relationships

Education helps individuals develop skills for communication, consent, and boundary-setting.

  • Better communication skills
  • Understanding of consent
  • Recognition of healthy relationships
